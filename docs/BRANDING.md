# BreachResponse: Master Brand Design

## A. Brand Core
- **One-line positioning**: The active immune system for Mantle smart contracts.
- **Brand personality**: Urgent, precise, authoritative, hyper-modern, and quietly powerful. It feels less like a "friendly AI assistant" and more like military-grade radar.
- **Target users**: Protocol developers, DeFi admins, multisig signers.
- **Emotional hook**: The adrenaline of an attack mixed with the relief of a 1-click rescue.

## B. Naming Options
1. **BreachResponse** (Current, strong, straightforward)
2. **Sentinel.ax** (Very Web3, authoritative)
3. **MantleAegis** (Ecosystem specific, mythological protection)
4. **0xGuardian** (Classic crypto feel)

## C. Recommended Brand Direction
**Sentinel.ax**
It sounds sharp, technical, and fast. The `.ax` extension gives it a hacker/security-ops vibe. For this document, we will use Sentinel.ax.

## D. Logo Direction
- **Concept**: A minimalist, geometric radar sweep that transforms into a cryptographic shield. 
- **Style**: Ultra-clean lines, monochromatic with a single glowing accent color. No complex gradients.
- **Typography pairing**: A brutalist sans-serif like `Space Grotesk` or `Inter Tight`.

## E. Color + Typography System
- **Background**: Deep Void Black (`#09090B`)
- **Surface Panels**: Dark Slate (`#18181B`)
- **Primary Accent (Safe/Active)**: Mantle Green/Neon Mint (`#10B981`)
- **Alert/Danger (Attack Detected)**: Cyber Red (`#EF4444`)
- **Typography**: `Space Grotesk` for headers (techy, monospaced feel) and `Inter` for data-dense tables/logs.

## F. Mascot Concept
- **Mascot Concept**: "The Watcher" (A sleek, abstract cybernetic owl or a floating geometric monolith). 
- **Personality**: Silent, highly observant. Only speaks when there's an anomaly.
- **Shape language**: Sharp angles, floating holographic elements.
- **Product integration**: Sits quietly in the top right of the dashboard. When an attack is detected, its eye glows Cyber Red and it expands to present the rescue transaction.

## G. Product UX Flow (The Hackathon Demo)
1. **Idle State**: The dashboard shows a heartbeat line of Mantle blocks. Green and calm.
2. **The Hack (Triggered via script)**: Sirens flash. The UI border turns Cyber Red. 
3. **Agent Intervention**: A modal slides in violently: "CRITICAL ANOMALY DETECTED. Reentrancy attempt on Vault.sol."
4. **The Rescue Formulation**: The Byreal Skills CLI output streams in real-time, building the `pause()` transaction payload.
5. **Human Approval**: The "Approve & Execute" button glows. The human clicks it. Wallet prompts for signature.
6. **Success State**: Screen flashes back to Mint Green. "Attack Thwarted. Funds Secured."

## H. Landing Page Direction
- **Hero section**: A dark, massive header saying "Don't just monitor. Defend." Background is a faint grid with occasional red pulses that are immediately neutralized by a green shield effect.
- **Call to Action**: "Connect Multisig"
- **Vibe**: Feels like the command center of a nuclear submarine.

## I. Pitch Deck Visual Direction
- Pitch deck must use a dark mode template. 
- Use high-contrast glowing lines against black backgrounds.
- Slide 1: The Problem (A timeline of a hack, showing the 3-hour human response gap).
- Slide 2: The Solution (Sentinel.ax filling that gap in 3 seconds).
- Slide 3: The Architecture (Showing Byreal Skills CLI interacting with Mantle).

## J. Image Generation Prompts
- **Logo**: `Minimalist vector logo for a cybersecurity protocol, an abstract geometric shield combined with a radar sweep, neon mint green glowing lines on a pure black background, flat design, modern, clean --v 6.0`
- **Mascot**: `A sleek cybernetic monolith drone floating in a dark room, glowing red mechanical eye, sharp edges, dark metallic texture, cyberpunk aesthetic, premium 3d render, dramatic lighting --ar 16:9`
- **Dashboard UI**: `UI design for a cybersecurity command center, dark mode, glowing red alerts, streaming data logs, a large 'Approve Transaction' button in neon green, high tech, minimalist brutalism, clean typography, dribbble, behance --ar 16:9`

## K. Final Creative Recommendation
Lean entirely into the "Command Center" aesthetic. Make the judges feel the panic of a hack and the power of the 1-click rescue. Keep the UI dark, the typography monospace, and the interactions explosive.
